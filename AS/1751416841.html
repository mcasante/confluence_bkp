Plataforma a55 : a55 Microservices: descriptions

Created by Tech A55 on Aug 12, 2021

Hubspot-manager: Management of all automatic hubspot data feed (from the platform > hubspot)

Auth-api: Control of new users registrations and their respective permissions

CRManager: Control of events (pub-sub) > for metrics generation

Client-manager: Management of customers (companies) and their respective risks classifications

Banking -api: Management of bank accounts and payment gateways in our data-base, both for credit the analysis and for the engagement after disbursement (keep track of the companies revenue)

Monitor-api: Management of credit operations already disbursed and their amortizations flow

Document-manager: Management of onboarding documents for each customer with an accepted proposal

Proposals-api: Management of credit proposals which are already generated > display to the client, expiration control, editing of terms, etc.)

Underwriting: Management of the credit operations formalization (includes the collection of onboarding data and documents for issuance of the CCB, in addition to being responsible for the integration with our formalization partners MoneyPlus and Socinal)

Oferta-maker: Proposal generation service (which feeds the proposals-api), using data obtained from public sources, as well as transactional data registered on the platform
