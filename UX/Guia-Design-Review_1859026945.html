UX : Guia Design Review

Created by Dani Lima on Nov 30, 2021

Este guia pode ser usado como guideline para as reuniões de review. Este formato pode ajudar a tornar os debates e troca de ideias mais produtivas. Copie e cole num documento ou em outra página do Confluence.

Problem statement:

What is the problem you want this design to solve?

Current system:

What are you currently doing (if anything) to solve this problem? What's the status quo?

Job to be done:

What will this new design solve for your users? What will it help them accomplish?

Business objective:

Why and how will this design benefit your business?

Metrics that matter:

What will you measure to determine how this new design is working? How?
