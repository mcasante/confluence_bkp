Guilherme Pettene Dantas : Memo

Created by Guilherme Pettene Dantas on May 20, 2022

  1. Executive Sumamry: Describe what this memo is about in one phrase

  2. Context and what should be done: Give a context of the problem, and what needs to be done in this moment.

    1. Which metric we address with this problem?

  3. What we know: Describe the outcome of your interviews, benchmarks and all the stuff you made to discover the problem

  4. What we don’t know: List the doubts that you still have and how are you preparing to deal with it

  5. Decisions made: List the decisions your team made, and why you made this decisions. You can list meetings. metrics and people to participate in each step of the process.

  6. Next Steps: What your team is doing next?

  7. Conclusion: When the the memo’s problem is solved

Memo’s objective: Keep track of the initiatives and decisions in an easy and accessible way, for all stakeholders.
